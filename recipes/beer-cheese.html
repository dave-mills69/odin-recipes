Beer Cheese

Rich Gourmet Beer Cheese

This is a wonderful pizza for those who are looking to get away from the basic, cheese and pepperoni pizza. Yum!!

Ingredients
  • ¾ (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 10 ounces processed cheese food (such as Velveeta®), cubed
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer, room temperature
  • 4 slices smoked Gouda cheese
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 4 slices smoked provolone cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish, or to taste
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons dry mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
Follow these instructions for perfect Beer Cheese
  1. Heat cream cheese and processed cheese food together in a sauce pan over low heat until melted, 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Slowly stir beer into cheese mixture until thoroughly mixed. Add Gouda cheese, mozzarella cheese, and provolone cheese to beer-cheese mixture, stirring continually until smooth, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Stir Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, horseradish, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper into cheese mixture until well mixed and heated through, about 10 more minutes.
